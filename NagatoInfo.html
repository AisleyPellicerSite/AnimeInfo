    Know more about the leader of Akatsuki

    Pain, also known as Nagato, is a central figure in the anime and manga series Naruto. He is the leader of the Akatsuki, a group of rogue ninja. Pain has a tragic backstory, as he grew up in the Hidden Rain Village during a time of war, which deeply shaped his worldview. After losing his family and friends due to the conflict, he became determined to end the cycle of pain and violence by seeking peace through power. Pain uses a unique ability called the "Six Paths of Pain," which allows him to control six bodies simultaneously, each with its own specific abilities. These bodies are actually reanimated corpses, with Nagato using chakra to control them through special piercings in their bodies. This gives him a significant advantage in combat, as he can utilize multiple powerful techniques at once. His ultimate goal was to create peace by forcibly controlling the world through his power, which he believed would prevent further suffering. This vision, however, was flawed, as he struggled with the nature of true peace and the consequences of his methods.
